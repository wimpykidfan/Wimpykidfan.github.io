AgTech 101

Agricultural Technology or 'AgTech' is exactly what it sounds like- the implementation of technology in agriculture!

In an increasingly technological world, it's only natural that one of the world's most valuable industries evolves alongside it. In recent years, soilless farming has quickly become one of the most efficient, profitable, and sustainable ways to grow crops due to its remarkable ability to produce higher yields in a shorter period of time.

Because of this, the Agtech industry is rapidly expanding. Both Tech and Agriculture are industries that are not going anywhere anytime soon, so now is as good a time as any to get involved in this new and exciting field.
