About Me

Hi! I'm Friday, I am a multi-media artist/designer and aspiring web-developer based in Queens, NY. This summer I joined a 10-week program with The Urban Wild called Intro to Agtech 101 as part of their fourth cohort. In this course, we received free hands-on, interdisciplinary job training skills in the realms of agriculture, technology, and construction as well as a $750 stipend to build our very own indoor smart farm! Using the skills and knowledge passed down to me by my cohort leaders and teammates, I've pieced together this website to show you my farm build process, what I've learned from my experience, and to hopefully inspire you to get started on your own indoor hydroponic system.
