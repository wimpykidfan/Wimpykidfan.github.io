Farm Design

When it came to the farm planning process we were provided with the information and guidance from our Agricultural Instructor Joshua to decide for ourselves which system would best suit our own individual needs. Some members of my cohort wanted to build a farm for a family member's personal use or business, while others planned on starting businesses themselves. From there, we were given complete creative control of our design. Personally, I was interested in cutting back on my grocery spending by growing some of my most purchased (and also most beginner-friendly crops); tomatoes and rainbow peppers. To spice things up I chose a variety I don't normally see in the grocery stores: sunsugar tomatoes (like the ones grown at Soulful Syngery) and mini rainbow bell peppers.

As for the farm build, I also went with what I deemed the most beginner friendly out of all the hydroponic systems: 2 deep water culture buckets.
